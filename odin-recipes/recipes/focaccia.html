Focaccia

You too, are capable of making beautiful bread!

My favorite type of carb, and a popular favourite for family, friends and co-workers! What they don't have to know, is how easy this is to make!

Ingredients

  • 7g of instant yeast
  • 370g of warm water
  • 500g of strong white bread flour
  • 10g salt
  • Garlic cloves, chopped into small chunks
  • Mixed dry herbs
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Add the warm water to the instant yeast in a large bowl and swish together with your fingers
  2. Add the flour and the salt to the bowl and mix together
  3. Leave for 30 minutes and enjoy your life
  4. Fold the dough in the bowl until the mixture feels stiff
  5. Cover the dough with clingfilm and leave for around 90 minutes
  6. Oil a baking tray and pour the dough into the tray. Stretch out with your fingers
  7. Preheat the over to 220C and let the dough rest for around 30 minutes
  8. Drizzle the dough with olive oil. Push your fingers into the dough to make dimples and sprinkle the garlic chunks and herbs over the dough
  9. Place the dough in the over for 25 minutes
  10. Let cool and enjoy!
